WHAT THIS WORK TAUGHT ME

Operating at $200M+ ARR scale with global checkout complexity sharpens design-engineering judgment in ways no early-stage shop can replicate. Insurance and checkout are the highest-stakes design surfaces in any travel business — small UX shifts move millions in revenue and millions more in customer protection. The work is conservative by necessity and ambitious in places it counts.

Three years here is the difference between "knows checkout" and "has shipped checkout that handles 100+ countries' worth of tax, currency, payment-method, and locale variation without breaking existing revenue."
