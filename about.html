About.

Raghav Ahuja. Design engineer, senior product designer, founder of JMNPR Labs. Brooklyn. Originally jamnapaar.

Thirteen years shipping conversion-critical product end-to-end — research, visual system, front-end, back-end, and the decisions that don't make it into tickets. Currently scaling insurance, checkout, and quoting at EF Education First, and building JMNPR Labs in my evenings.

The studio is named Jamanapaar — Hindi for Jamuna paar, the other side of the Yamuna. East Delhi, where I grew up, and the side of the river outsiders looked down on. Brooklyn, where I live now, across a different river. Both sides of a river, both the side that had to build its own tools. The studio is named for the posture.

what I care about
  • §Craft over feature-count. The tool that does one thing beautifully beats the tool that does nine things grudgingly.
  • §Self-serve over sales. Price it, ship it, let the work sell itself. Sales motion kills catalog velocity.
  • §Global from day one. PPP pricing. Multiple languages. The creator in Jakarta and the creator in Jamnapaar are not an afterthought.
  • §AI amplifies, never authors. Every tool respects the human's voice. No one-click generation of human creative work. Ever.
  • §Standards grow the category. Publishing open formats and free tools seeds every paid conversation.
