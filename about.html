Frameworks I've authored

  • Confidence-to-Book (CtB) — a five-stage mental model that plots where an advisor-assisted prospect sits between curiosity and committed booking. Maps doubts and next-best-actions at each stage. Drove the +10% insurance redesign and is now the shared vocabulary for every advisor-flow review at EF.
  • MIND — a synthesis method for mixed-method research (M: mine raw inputs · I: inventory themes · N: name the tensions · D: decide the bet). Turns a week of interviews into a one-page artefact a team can argue against. Used across the design org to unblock stuck projects.
