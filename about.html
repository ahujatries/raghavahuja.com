about.

design engineer. brooklyn, by way of east delhi and mumbai. fifteen years of shipping inside other people's roadmaps; now mostly shipping inside my own.

I design and build the whole loop — discovery, the figma, the codebase, the shipped url, the post-launch instrumentation. The role most teams call design engineer or design systems lead; some call it founding designer. The label matters less than the loop.

For the last three years I've been at EF World Journeys, working on the booking surface — checkout, insurance, the quoting tool — and authoring two design frameworks (MIND and Designing for Humans) that the org uses across surfaces. Before that, two years at Zulily on a personalization engine that sent 16M unique emails a day. Before that, four years as creative director at The Social Booth in Mumbai, running a 10-person team across 20+ enterprise clients including Ducati and Audi.

On the side I'm building JMNPR Labs — a one-person tools studio, named for Jamanapaar, the other side of the Yamuna. Arqo is the consumer surface; five more flagships are on deck.
